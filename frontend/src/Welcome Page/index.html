About Us

To provide students with a vibrant environment that encourages self-discovery and development, nurturing service oriented leadership. Clubs and societies are a platform for student personal development and collaboration with both Strathmore university departments and corporates future mutual benefit.

Objectivies

Strathmore University strives to provide, teach and facilitate students with different opportunities at the school to enhance and education and boost students careers.

  • 01 To provide leadership opportunities for students.

    In sports and societies, providing leadership opportunities for students means creating opportunities for them to take on leadership roles and responsibilities within their teams or groups. This can include positions such as team captain, club president, or event coordinator. By giving students these leadership opportunities, they can develop important skills such as communication, organization, and teamwork, while also contributing to the success of their team or group.

  • 02 To provide opportunities for students to meet future employers.

    To provide opportunities for students to meet future employers through sports and societies, events such as career fairs, industry-specific workshops, and networking events can be organized. These events can help students build connections with professionals in their field of interest and learn about potential job or internship opportunities. By attending these events and developing relationships with professionals, students can increase their chances of finding employment in their desired field.

  • 03 To provide an opportunity for self-discovery and personal development.

    In sports and societies, providing an opportunity for self-discovery and personal development means creating an environment where students can explore their interests, develop new skills, and challenge themselves. This can be achieved through participation in various activities such as clubs, teams, or volunteer work. By engaging in these activities, students can discover their passions, build confidence, and develop important life skills that will benefit them in their personal and professional lives.

  • 04 To provide an opportunity for learning communication skills.

    In sports and societies, providing an opportunity for learning communication skills means creating an environment where students can practice and develop their abilities to express themselves effectively. This can be achieved through participation in various activities such as debates, public speaking events, or group projects. By engaging in these activities, students can improve their ability to articulate their thoughts, collaborate with others, and build relationships through effective communication.

  • 05 To provide an opportunity to practice whatever has been learnt in class.

    In sports and societies, providing an opportunity to practice whatever has been learnt in class means creating an environment where students can apply the knowledge and skills they have gained through their academic studies. This can be achieved through activities such as research projects, competitions, or community service projects that are related to their field of study. By engaging in these activities, students can deepen their understanding of their subject matter, gain practical experience, and build their resume for future career opportunities.

  • 06 To learn how to work as a team and deliver on targets.

    In sports and societies, learning how to work as a team and deliver on targets means creating an environment where students can develop their ability to collaborate with others towards a shared goal. This can be achieved through participation in activities such as team sports, group projects, or event planning. By engaging in these activities, students can learn how to communicate effectively, delegate tasks, and work towards common objectives. This can help them develop essential teamwork and leadership skills that will be valuable in both their personal and professional lives.

  • 07 Facilitate students make linkages with employers.

    In sports and societies, facilitating students to make linkages with employers involves creating opportunities for students to connect with professionals and industry leaders. This can be achieved through events such as career fairs, guest speaker sessions, or industry-specific workshops. By engaging in these activities, students can expand their network, gain valuable insights into their chosen field, and explore potential job or internship opportunities. This can help them to establish themselves as strong candidates in the job market and secure employment in their desired field.
